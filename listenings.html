IT Career Podcast

This podcast is very informative and is always framed in a way that is easy to understand for people who are interested in transitioning into an IT career. The host The Bearded I.T Dad is inspiring for me as he mentions often in his interviews that he came from a construction background driving trucks for a living, making good money over $70k a year but found his love and passion in I.T. His story and message during podcasts is always to encourage listeners to overcome that fear of making the switch to an IT profession.
